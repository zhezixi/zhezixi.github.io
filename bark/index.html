The
"BARK"
Parking App

 

Project Goal

Approaching a more convenient, easier, parking experience.

Project context

BARK is a crowdsourcing-based parking application to boost the efficiency for customers in reporting parking attendant, reservation, and quick checking in/out by QR code. I started this GROUP project with my classmates in Dec 2018, Dr.Wyche's prototyping class. Our main goal is to improve the parking situation around our campus by improving parking experience.
During the assigned developing period, which was less than a week, we only had a very rough product finished. After the last final, I decided to continue revising it until it meets my expectation.

Revised Sitemap

In order to gave bark better structures, I decided to build a sitemap. It splits Bark to 5 pieces which are reserving spots, QR code parking, my profile, find parking and report parking attendent.

Report parking attendent

1)The report button is on the right side of each interface which is easier for users to report parking attendents without much efforts.
2）The positioning system can automatically update the current location so that the users only need to spend two seconds to complete the reporting action.

Recieve the update

After recieve the report, this app have 3 ways to notify their user:
1)The timer page
2)The lockscreen page
3)The police icon on the map in the mainpage.
The lock screen page provides express payment pathway by simply tapping the notification, giving our users more chances to pay their parking before they get ticket from parking attendents.

Reserve spots

1）At right side of searching bar, users can change the time period they want to reserve their car.
2)Using balance as default payment, users can change their payment method by click the small blue button on the top of the payment page.
3)Visualizing the reserve parking page would remind the users more easily on finding their vehicles.

QR code parking

1）The QR code feature provides quick check-in and check-out experiences.
2) Giving users a choice to authorize their payment later.
3)The parking timer page not only gives our users the exact time they parked, but also provides notifications about parking attendents for users to pay their parking before getting any tickets.

User profile

1) I designed the navigation on the side due to more understandable layout purpose.
2)Users can add their liscenced plates of their vehicles in our app. After they using QR Code parking or reservation. the default car plate will automatically appear on the payment and parking timer page. Also, users are able to change their plates after they check in.
3)Users can use credit card and balance to complete their payments.They also can add new cards by simply scan their cards.
