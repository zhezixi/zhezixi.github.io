"Flying Kitchen"


-The first time approach of UI/UX design.

 

Project Goal

Making a faster, safer and cheapr take-out food experience with drone delivery.

Project context

Flying kitchen is a phone app that can support drone delivery. I started this individual project in OCT 2018, Dr.Wyche's prototyping class. It perfecly sloves all problems we are faceing, by providing lower-cost delivery, avoidance of traffic situations and faster experiences.

Involvement

UI/UX design
User Testing
Product management

Date

Oct 2018

Tools

Adobe XD
design bold

Instructor

Susan Wyche, Professor of MI420

Difficulties

In order to have a better understanding of Drone delivery, I personally found three participants, including two MSU students and one non student. All of them are drone lovers. I intervewed them about their takeout food experiences, also asked about their concerns and desires about the drone delivery.

Initial Design

We narrowed it down to 4 ideas about this drone delivery app:
1) Using different colors and codes of boxes to make it recognizable.
2) Using QR code to open metal boxes to maintain a safer, easier and faster experience.
3)Using filters to make it eaiser to know different restaurants' delivery methods.
4)real-time updates of drone's location.

Paper Prototype

After I evaluated my paper prototype, I acknowledged that there were too many features inside the mainpage which could cause some confusions. Therefore, I should choose the features inside the navigation more carefully.

Low-fidelity Prototype

1）I simplified the payment process, decided to use QR code for better and quicker delivery pick up.
2)I added more features in filter functions, including prices, delivery methods, store's open or close satus and locations.
3)I also simplified the main page, deleted the redundant user profile and the search in the map function.

High-fidelity Prototype

1）I changed the color from low-saturated blue to high-saturated pink to create visual attraction.
2) Adding password features to pick up our delivery just in case that the QR code function not working properly.
3）Increasing the element size in the mainpage, splitting it into 4 parts: Searching restaurants, recent order (restaurants), popular restaurants and the navigation.
4)Changing the navigation more visually attractive, and also the elements inside the navigation to acheive better experiences.
