Flying Kitchen

 

-The first time approach of UI/UX design.

Project Goal

Making a faster, safer and cheaper take out food experiences

Project context

Flying kitchen is a phone app that can support drone delivery. I started this individual project in OCT 2018, Dr.Whyche's prototyping class. It perfecly sloved all problems we faced, including lower cost by drone delivery, avoid traffic situations and faster experiences.

Involvement

UI/UX design
User Research
User Testing
Product management

Date

Oct 2018

Tools

Adobe XD
design bold

Instructor

Susan Wyche, Professor of MI420

Contextual Interviews & Persona development

In order to have a better understanding of Drone delivery, I personally found three participants, including two MSU students and one non student. All of them are drone lovers. I intervewed them about their take out food experiences, also asked about their concerns and desires about the drone delivery.

Initial Design

We narrow down 4 ideas about this drone delivery app:
1) Using Different colors and code of boxes to make it recognizable.
2) Using QR code to open metal boxes to maintain a safer, easier and faster experiences.
3)Using filters make it eaiser to know different restaurant's delivery methods.
4)real-time updates of drone's location

Paper Prototype

After i evaluated my paper prototype, i acknowledge that there were too many features inside the mainpage which make users confuse. Also i should more carefully choose features inside the navigation
